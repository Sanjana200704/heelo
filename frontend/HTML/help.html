Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use the YouTube Summarizer?

Simply paste a YouTube video URL into the input box on the home page and click the Summarize button. Your summary will appear below in both paragraph and bullet-point formats.

2. Can I access my previous summaries?

Yes, you can view your history by clicking on the History link in the menu.

3. How do I change my preferences?

Navigate to the Settings page from the menu to adjust your summarization style, theme, and more.

4. What languages are supported for translation?

Currently, the app supports translation to English. Additional languages may be added in future updates.
